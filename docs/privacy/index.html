    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: May 19, 2026

    This Privacy Policy describes how DSP Guide ("we", "our", or "the Site") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website at dsp-guide.com.

    Information We Collect

    Google Analytics

    We use Google Analytics (GA4), a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and analyze information about how visitors use the Site. This information may include:

    • Pages visited and time spent on each page
    • Referring website or search terms
    • Browser type, device type, and operating system
    • Approximate geographic location (country/city level)
    • IP address (anonymized where possible)

    This data is collected anonymously and used to improve the Site's content and user experience. Google may use the data collected to contextualize and personalize ads of its own advertising network.

    For more information on Google Analytics data processing, see Google's Privacy Policy and Google Analytics Terms of Service.

    Google AdSense

    We display ads via Google AdSense, which uses cookies and web beacons to serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing history and interests. AdSense may collect:

    • Your IP address
    • Browser and device information
    • Pages you have visited on this and other websites
    • Click and impression data for ads

    You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

    Cookies

    Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. We use the following types of cookies:

    | Type | Purpose | Duration | |------|---------|----------| | Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) | Understand how visitors interact with the Site | Up to 2 years | | Advertising cookies (Google AdSense) | Serve relevant ads and measure ad performance | Session to 1 year | | Essential cookies | Ensure basic site functionality | Session |

    Third-Party Services

    We use the following third-party services:

    • Google Analytics — anonymous usage analytics
    • Google AdSense — contextual and personalized advertising
    • GitHub Pages — web hosting; see GitHub's Privacy Statement

    These third parties have their own privacy policies governing the use of your data. We encourage you to review them.

    Your Choices

    • Opt out of Google Analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
    • Opt out of personalized ads: Visit Google Ad Settings
    • Manage cookies: Adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies. Note that doing so may affect site functionality.

    Data Retention

    Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months. Ad impression data is retained as per Google's standard retention policies.

    Contact

    If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email associated with the Site.

    Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
