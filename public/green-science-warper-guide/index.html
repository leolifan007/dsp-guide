Green Science and Warpers: Stop Being Scared of Green Matrix (DSP Guide)

Updated: May 14, 2026
<p>You've got Yellow Science running. Titanium flowing. Everything is… fine. Then you look at Green Matrix. Three ingredients. Processors. Casimir Crystals. Electromagnetic Turbines.</p> <p>And you think: <strong>&quot;Yeah no. I'll do it later.&quot;</strong></p> <p>I get it. Every DSP player hits this wall. But here's the thing — Green Matrix unlocks Space Warpers. And Warpers turn your factory from a solar-system-only operation into a galactic empire. You want that.</p> <div class="pro-tip"><strong>TL;DR</strong> — Green Matrix needs 3 things: <strong>Processors + Casimir Crystals + EM Turbines.</strong> Scale your existing Processor line. Build a Casimir plant. That's most of the work done.</div> <hr> <div class="section-head"><h2>The Three Things You Need</h2></div> <p><strong>Green Matrix = Processors + Casimir Crystals + EM Turbines</strong></p> <div class="game-row"><div class="game-slot"><span class="game-qty">1x</span><img src="/dsp-guide/images/dsp-icons/processor.png" alt="processor" class="game-img" loading="lazy"><span class="game-name">Processor</span></div><span class="game-plus">+</span><div class="game-slot"><span class="game-qty">1x</span><img src="/dsp-guide/images/dsp-icons/casimir-crystal.png" alt="casimir" class="game-img" loading="lazy"><span class="game-name">Casimir</span></div><span class="game-plus">+</span><div class="game-slot"><span class="game-qty">1x</span><img src="/dsp-guide/images/dsp-icons/mag-turbine.png" alt="em-turbine" class="game-img" loading="lazy"><span class="game-name">Em turbine</span></div><span class="game-plus">&rarr;</span><div class="game-slot result"><span class="game-qty">1x</span><img src="/dsp-guide/images/dsp-icons/g-matrix.png" alt="green-matrix" class="game-img" loading="lazy"><span class="game-name">Green matrix</span></div></div> <hr> <h3 id="1-processors-you-already-make-these">1. Processors (You Already Make These)</h3> <p>If you're at Yellow Science, you already have a processor line. You just need more. A green matrix build running 3 labs/min needs ~4 processors per second. That's about <strong>20-25 assemblers</strong> making processors.</p> <p><strong>Don't build a new factory.</strong> Just expand your existing one. Slap down more assemblers on your current circuit board + silicon line.</p> <div class="game-row"><div class="game-slot"><span class="game-qty">1x</span><img src="/dsp-guide/images/dsp-icons/circuit-board.png" alt="circuit" class="game-img" loading="lazy"><span class="game-name">Circuit</span></div><span class="game-plus">+</span><div class="game-slot"><span class="game-qty">1x</span><img src="/dsp-guide/images/dsp-icons/silicium-high-purity.png" alt="silicon" class="game-img" loading="lazy"><span class="game-name">Silicon</span></div><span class="game-plus">&rarr;</span><div class="game-slot result"><span class="game-qty">1x</span><img src="/dsp-guide/images/dsp-icons/processor.png" alt="processor" class="game-img" loading="lazy"><span class="game-name">Processor</span></div></div> <hr> <h3 id="2-casimir-crystals-the-scary-one">2. Casimir Crystals (The &quot;Scary&quot; One)</h3> <p>Casimir Crystals need three ingredients:</p> <div class="game-row"><div class="game-slot"><span class="game-qty">1x</span><img src="/dsp-guide/images/dsp-icons/hydrogen.png" alt="hydrogen" class="game-img" loading="lazy"><span class="game-name">Hydrogen</span></div><span class="game-plus">+</span><div class="game-slot"><span class="game-qty">1x</span><img src="/dsp-guide/images/dsp-icons/graphene.png" alt="graphene" class="game-img" loading="lazy"><span class="game-name">Graphene</span></div><span class="game-plus">+</span><div class="game-slot"><span class="game-qty">2x</span><img src="/dsp-guide/images/dsp-icons/titanium-plate.png" alt="titanium" class="game-img" loading="lazy"><span class="game-name">Titanium</span></div><span class="game-plus">&rarr;</span><div class="game-slot result"><span class="game-qty">1x</span><img src="/dsp-guide/images/dsp-icons/casimir-crystal.png" alt="casimir" class="game-img" loading="lazy"><span class="game-name">Casimir</span></div></div> <p><strong>The hydrogen is the real constraint.</strong> You need a steady stream — way more than your refineries produce.</p> <p><strong>The fix:</strong> Find a gas giant in your system.</p> <img src="/dsp-guide/images/orbital-collector-h2.svg" alt="Orbital Collectors for Hydrogen" class="schema-diagram" loading="lazy" width="700"> <p>Build an <strong>Orbital Collector</strong> on it. Each collector pulls <strong>0.35 hydrogen/second</strong> forever. Drop 10 collectors around a gas giant and you'll never worry about hydrogen again.</p> <div class="pro-tip"><strong>Pro Tip</strong> — Drop 20 collectors around your nearest gas giant. Not 10. You'll thank yourself later when purple science needs ridiculous amounts of hydrogen too.</div> <hr> <h3 id="3-em-turbines-easy">3. EM Turbines (Easy)</h3> <p>These are just electric motors + magnetic coils:</p> <div class="game-row"><div class="game-slot"><span class="game-qty">1x</span><img src="/dsp-guide/images/dsp-icons/electric-motor.png" alt="motor" class="game-img" loading="lazy"><span class="game-name">Motor</span></div><span class="game-plus">+</span><div class="game-slot"><span class="game-qty">1x</span><img src="/dsp-guide/images/dsp-icons/magnetism-wire.png" alt="coil" class="game-img" loading="lazy"><span class="game-name">Coil</span></div><span class="game-plus">&rarr;</span><div class="game-slot result"><span class="game-qty">1x</span><img src="/dsp-guide/images/dsp-icons/mag-turbine.png" alt="em-turbine" class="game-img" loading="lazy"><span class="game-name">Em turbine</span></div></div> <p>If you've been automating motors at all, you already have most of this. Build a few more assemblers. Done.</p> <div class="pro-tip"><strong>Why are you scared of green?</strong> The truth: it's not harder than yellow. Yellow asks you to handle oil, hydrogen, graphite, and titanium all at once across multiple planets. Green is just &quot;more of the same but with processors.&quot; You've got this.</div> <hr> <div class="section-head"><h2>The Warpers Payoff</h2></div> <p>Once Green Matrix is running, your research unlocks: <strong>Space Warper</strong>.</p> <p><strong>Space Warper = your vessels can fly to other star systems.</strong> Your factory goes from one system to unlimited.</p> <img src="/dsp-guide/images/warper-payoff.svg" alt="Warper interstellar travel benefit" class="schema-diagram" loading="lazy" width="700"> <p>Each warper costs <strong>1 Green Matrix</strong>. Each vessel trip costs <strong>1 warper</strong>. One warper moves <strong>200 items</strong>. That's insanely efficient.</p> <p><strong>Build a warper production line right next to your green matrix setup.</strong> Route a fraction of your green matrix output into Assembling Machines M2/M3 that produce warpers. Feed them into an ILS. Set ILS slot to Supply.</p> <p>Now every ILS in your network can grab warpers and start shipping across the galaxy.</p> <div class="pro-tip"><strong>One gotcha:</strong> ILS ships won't pick up warpers unless you set a slot to Supply for warpers.
